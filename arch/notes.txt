## fix for R8188 wifi dongle driver

r8188eu driver module has lots of issues. better use rtl8188eu
disable rtl8188eu

and add following file
cat /etc/modprobe.d/nor8188eu.conf
blacklist r8188eu
modprobe rtl8188eu
