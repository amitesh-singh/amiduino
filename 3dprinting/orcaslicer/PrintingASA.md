# printing ASA on A1

A1 is in an enclosure and it really prints well even during winter.

Keys things to consider before printing it.

- use numakers buildplate. the best plate 
- set the bed temperature slowly in the step of 5
- print the first layer slowly. 20 mm for the border and 50 mm for the infill
- in case of support, use strong tree support with top and bottom distance to be 0.1 or 0.12.
